Northern Border


Celine Dion came from the city of Quebec Canada and was born to an impoverished but what was later to be said happy family. Her parents owned a small piano bar where Celine would watch performers nightly and later said that she often dreamed of becoming a performer herself. She would start singing for patrons at an early age as well as performing on the piano and would go on to compose her first song at the age of twelve under the guidance of her mother.
