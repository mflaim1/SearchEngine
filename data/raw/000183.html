Matt Lucas (I)

Actor | Writer | Producer
Matt Lucas was born on March 5, 1974 in Paddington, London, England as Matthew Richard Lucas. He is an actor and writer, known for Alice im Wunderland (2010), Brautalarm (2011) and Little Britain (2003). He was previously married to Kevin McGee. See full bio »
